History

PataMtandao started in the year 2010. This was after board members saw that technology was growing fast and their was need to come up with a soluton and provide members with cheap internet. This was because the existing Mobile Provider companies had expensive mobile buddles. By 2015, people had started working from home, too many online jobs had flowed in the market. These made a necesity for people to have internet at home.
PataMtandao is an upcoming Internet Providing company that aims at offering cheap and affordable internet connection at pocket friendly prices. Our target is to ensure that almost every house hold has a network connection.
